Top stories.

Air Canada flights will resume on Sunday evening after the Canadian government intervened to end a strike by ten thousand flight attendants. Federal Jobs Minister Patty Hajdu ordered attendants back to work less than twelve hours after they walked off the job, citing the broader economic impact.

President Donald Trump walked into a summit with Russia's Vladimir Putin pressing for a ceasefire deal in Ukraine but instead dropped his demand in favor of pursuing a full peace accord. According to reports, Trump's new position aligns with Putin's. European leaders, including German Chancellor Friedrich Merz and French President Emmanuel Macron, will join Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelenskyy during his visit to Washington on Monday.

In China, at least ten people died in a flash flood in Inner Mongolia, with two others still missing, according to News18. Over seven hundred people are searching. Severe weather has caused significant damage across the country.
