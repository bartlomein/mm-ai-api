Bangladesh has denied India's proposal to facilitate Indian goods' transit through Bangladesh en route to the United States due to concerns over high U.S. tariffs on re-exported Indian-origin goods. The U.S. tariffs are imposed based on the origin of the exporter, not modified by transit or re-export. Additionally, Bangladesh officials noted that the country lacks the necessary industrial infrastructure to perform significant transformation on Indian goods that would reclassify them as Bangladeshi origin.
